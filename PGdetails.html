Paying Guest (PG) accommodations are popular among students and professionals seeking affordable housing options.
 However, residing in a PG often comes with a set of rules and regulations designed to ensure a harmonious living environment.
  Common rules include:

Payment Terms:

  • Advance Deposit: Typically, PGs require a security deposit equivalent to one month's rent, paid in advance. This deposit safeguards the property against potential damages or unpaid dues.

    • NORMSCONFERENCE.COM

  • Prepaid Rent: Rent is often expected to be paid before the start of each month to ensure timely payments.

Food Preferences:

  • Dietary Restrictions: Some PGs may have specific food policies, such as serving only vegetarian meals. In such cases, cooking or consuming non-vegetarian food on the premises might be prohibited.

    • ZOLO STAYS

Curfew Hours:

  • Fixed Timings: To ensure safety and order, many PGs enforce specific return times in the evening or night. It's essential to be aware of these timings to avoid inconveniences.

    • NORMSCONFERENCE.COM

Visitor Policies:

    >
  • Guest Restrictions: Generally, PGs do not allow friends or visitors to stay overnight. Additionally, members of the opposite gender are typically restricted from entering rooms to maintain a comfortable environment for all residents.

    • ZOLO STAYS

Prohibited Activities:

  • No Parties: To maintain peace, loud parties and gatherings are usually not permitted within PG premises.

    • NORMSCONFERENCE.COM

  • Substance Restrictions: The consumption of alcohol, smoking, and possession of addictive substances are strictly prohibited. Violations can lead to immediate expulsion and possible legal action.

    • GPGS.IN

Cleanliness and Maintenance:

  • Room Upkeep: Residents are expected to keep their rooms clean and tidy. While regular housekeeping might be provided, individual responsibility is emphasized
    • KRCPG.COM

  • No Alterations: Affixing or displaying photos, posters, or other items on the walls may be prohibited to maintain the aesthetics of the premises.

    • GPGS.IN

Security Measures:

  • D Submission: Upon arrival, residents may be required to submit copies of their ID and a passport-sized photo. Some PGs might also request identification from parents or local guardians.

    • KRCPG.COM
  • Limited Access: Entry to the PG premises is often restricted to authorized individuals only. Unauthorized visitors are generally not allowed.
    •  WORLDBLAZE

Notice Period:

  • Vacating Notice: If a resident plans to move out, they are typically required to inform the management at least 30 days in advance.

    • GPGS.IN
